Kids And Students

"Service Beyond Self"

Our School Motto — guiding us since 2010

DPSI Ghana commenced in the year 2010, aiming for quality education and learning for excellence, providing holistic, modern and comprehensive education beginning from Creche class through A-Level — integrating technology-driven classrooms.

With 'Service Beyond Self' as the motto, DPSI Tema caters to the needs of children of Ghana and neighbouring countries — offering a truly international learning environment.
